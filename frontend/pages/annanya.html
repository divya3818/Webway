About Ananya

Our Cultural Heritage

Ananya is our college's annual cultural festival that celebrates the rich diversity of Indian traditions and arts. The name "Ananya" means unique and unparalleled, reflecting our commitment to showcasing exceptional talent.

First started in 2010, Ananya has grown to become one of the most anticipated cultural events in the region, attracting participants from colleges across the state.

This three-day extravaganza features a wide range of events including dance, music, theater, art exhibitions, and traditional craft workshops.

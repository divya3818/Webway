About Flashmob

Our college flashmob is a spontaneous performance that brings together students from various departments to create a memorable experience. Organized by the Cultural Committee, these flashmobs are designed to showcase talent, promote unity, and add an element of surprise to campus life.

Participation is open to all students, regardless of dance experience. Regular practices are held before events to ensure everyone is comfortable with the routine.

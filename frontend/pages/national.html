About National Events

National events are occasions of great significance for India, celebrated collectively by its people to honor history, culture, achievements, and national unity.

These events play an important role in strengthening patriotism, reminding citizens of the sacrifices made for freedom, and celebrating the democratic values of the nation.

At SPHURTI, we commemorate these events with official ceremonies, cultural programs, flag hoisting, and enthusiastic participation from our college community.

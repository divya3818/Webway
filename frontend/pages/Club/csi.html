About CSI

The Coders Club Flashmob is a unique event that combines technology and creativity in a surprising performance. Organized by the Coders Club, this flashmob brings together students passionate about coding to create a memorable experience that showcases our technical skills in a fun, artistic way.

No prior dance experience is required - we focus on simple moves that everyone can learn. Regular practice sessions are held to ensure everyone feels confident and comfortable with the routine.

This event is not just about performance; it's about building community, breaking stereotypes about coders, and having fun together!
